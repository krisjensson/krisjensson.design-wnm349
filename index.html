Dashboard

With the dashboard we do everything we can to make it as simple for you as possible. Here you can see everything that is going on with your beehive. We make sure that if there is something wrong you can notice it right away and with the option to push in notifications.

Strength

The strength of a hive refers to the number of bees in the hive. A hive consists of one or more compartments, called supers. A strong colony has about 15.000 - 20.000 bees in each super. With our traffic sensor in the bottom boarder we can estimate the bee population.

Harvest

When you want to enjoy your honey you simply empty a frame and the honey will flow down into your container. The container has weight sensors so you can see how full it is. To empty the container we built in fingerscan tap so the bears stay away.

Condition

The most importand aspect for the bee broods is temperature. One of the reason why were loosing bees is because of weather conditions. Aiza makes sure that you can see and change the humidity or temperature of your beehive through the app.

Frames

Where the action happens. In your upper super you have six frames all with different colour. Each frame has weight sensor so you can now how much honey the bees have made. When it is time we will let you know and you can empty the frame into the container.

My Yard

With my yard you can change the colour of your beehive and also add UV glow. The colours are the bees three favorites blue, violet and purple. We recommend the first three weeks you keep the colour the same after that time you can start playing a little bit.
